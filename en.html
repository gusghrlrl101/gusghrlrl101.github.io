Configuration of AI Platform Serving Environment

Designed and developed an automated environment for real-time serving of AI models.

Woowa Bros.
June 2023 – June 2024

ChallengesPreviously, there was no established environment for serving AI models in real-time (via APIs), which required many tasks to be performed manually. With numerous upcoming projects requiring real-time requests, the need for an automated serving environment became apparent. I proposed and initiated this project to address the issue.
SolutionsUsing BentoML, I built a simple and versatile real-time serving environment capable of supporting multiple frameworks. I integrated GitLab CI with ArgoCD to automate the deployment process. Additionally, I established a systematic version management policy and implemented a rapid rollback system to enhance stability. I also automated the creation of monitoring dashboards for each service and integrated an error tracking system to strengthen monitoring capabilities.
OutcomesThe serving process was reduced by 40%, significantly decreasing engineering resource demands and accelerating development speed. This allowed for faster adoption of services. Currently, 30 real-time services are stably handling around 2 billion requests per month, with an average peak of 5,000 requests per second.

Configuration of AI Platform Training/Batch Environment

Designed and developed an environment to simplify the development of AI model training and batch processing tasks.

Woowa Bros.
June 2022 – June 2023

ChallengesPreviously, the lack of AI training and batch processing environments led to decreased efficiency. A solution was needed to address issues such as data management, pipeline construction, and environmental consistency.
SolutionsI developed commonly used features as libraries and introduced a data management policy to improve development convenience. I also created a system where batch services could be easily generated by writing YAML files, enabling even non-developers to collaborate efficiently. Furthermore, I simplified the development process by aligning development and production environments using tools like MLFlow and Docker.
OutcomesReduced the training and batch process by 30% and 67%, respectively, significantly enhancing development speed and operational efficiency. Currently, 130 batch services are being managed stably.

Unmanned Convenience Store Project

Designed and developed an environment for integrating and inferring multiple AI models.

Kakao & Kakao Enterprise
June 2019 – June 2022

ChallengesI was tasked with independently designing and building an integrated system for team research projects (e.g. human detection, pose estimation, object tracking). The system needed to support inference in two environments: edge devices and servers.
SolutionsI achieved over a 3x speed improvement on edge devices by utilizing TensorRT conversion and CUDA acceleration, enabling real-time processing. For real-time video input, I accelerated processing with DeepStream and managed multiple camera inputs simultaneously. On the server side, I used infrastructures like Kafka, Redis, and InfluxDB to seamlessly connect data across modules.
OutcomesThe company’s convenience store was transformed into an unmanned store, allowing real-time tracking of customer movements and purchased items. I developed a system capable of calculating and tracking real-time 3D coordinates using multiple standard cameras without relying on 3D cameras.
